Social Media Graphics

Created graphics for social campaigns and posts for Planned Parenthood.



This is a selection of some of the social media graphics created for standalone social media posts during my time at Planned Parenthood.

Most of these were made in a few minutes in order to react to breaking news in a timely manner. I created and maintained a large library of assets that I could re-use in a pinch.

It can be fun to work on a project for weeks at a time, but sometimes there's no substitute for the immediacy of social media.
