About me

I've worked as a designer for six years. I like to make things. Pretty things. Functional things. Things that make people want to click to see more. I create data-driven designs that work. A big idea is only as good as its execution.

In my free time I work on my illustration skills and manage my online shop. I've created a few product lines and enjoy crunching numbers to figure out what to make next. Ask to see my spreadsheets.

When I'm not behind a screen, I'm out hiking, running, or eating my way across all seven continents. Three down, Four to go. I would probably have crossed more off that list, but I can't seem to stop going back to Galway, Ireland.
