Amanda's Center Branding

Amanda's Center is a division of the Fayette County Sheriff's Office that aids victims of domestic violence. In the fall of 2012 I designed a brochure, journal, and logo for the center using the art and journals of a victim of domestic violence.

It was important to create a sense of peace throughout the literature for this project. The small plant evokes a calm feeling and the possibility of new growth.

Project finished in Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign (CS5)
