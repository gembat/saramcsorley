FCSO Website

The Fayette County Sheriff's Office's website had not been updated since 2008 when I was brought on in late 2011 to redesign it. The site is simple, clean, and structured in a way that makes information easy to find to get users to what they are looking for.

Project finished in Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign (CS5)
