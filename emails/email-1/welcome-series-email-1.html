Welcome

Thank you for connecting with PATH!

At PATH, we believe everyone deserves an equal chance for a healthy life. That’s what drives us to find ingenious solutions to the world’s most challenging problems—and to take them to scale to save and improve hundreds of millions of lives every year.

But we can’t do it alone. We partner with governments, industry, and fellow innovators and makers to solve enormous problems—and we rely on support from friends like you.

We are technologists, leading with data and facts; collaborating with scientists, industry experts, thought leaders, and tech influencers—join our networks on Twitter, Instagram, and Facebook.

We are innovators, driven to find solutions to global health challenges—from vaccine injection to female condom adoption to fighting malaria.

We are community-based, driving sustainable change by partnering at a grassroots level. Join us on a PATH Journey to see how we work.

We are advocates, influencing health policy and advocating for health equity globally and at within communities. Invest in PATH to help us bring good health within reach for everyone.
Thank you for your support,

Steve
President and CEO
