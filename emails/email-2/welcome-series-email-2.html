We save lives using these 7 everyday items

One of PATH’s superpowers is seeing the potential to turn ordinary things into extraordinary lifesavers.

1. Bike pump

The RELI Delivery System harnesses the air power of an ordinary bicycle hand pump. With a few minutes of pumping, the device delivers hours of lifesaving intravenous medication or nutrition to people in places with no electricity.
