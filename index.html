PATH 40th Totes

Product design from concept through production.

For PATH's 40th anniversary, I designed a tote bag for each of PATH's 1500 employees. The Chinese PATH office had printed bags for a party they threw to celebrate the 40th anniversary, so it was decided we would use the same factory to create the bags, but design a bag that would be a little more trendy.

I worked with a translator in PATH's China office to work with the factory to choose a fabric type and color, and to create a screen printed design that would adhere well to the bag. I created different designs based on the PATH 40th logo bug, and the minimalist "confetti" design was chosen from the bunch. The bags are through production and currently headed their way to PATH offices all around the world.

Teen Council Rebrand

T-Shirts & Swag.

Teen Council is Planned Parenthood’s award-winning peer education program. Teen Council empowers teens to educate their peers about healthy sexuality and relationship issues. After their logo redesign, they needed new t-shirts and sweatshirts to wear while working as sex educators. The shirts needed to be professional, but also cool enough for high school aged-students to wear and look up to.

The shirts and stickers were intended to promote the new logo, and create buy-in among the teen council members.

Ultimately, we decided to go with a single color t-shirt design, and let the teen council members choose one of the 3 colors in the logo, or charcoal as their shirt color. When they're all together, the colors in the logo are visible and cohesive, but without the price tag of the four color shirts.

SRKW Poster

Data visualization for demographic info. And orcas!

Using information gathered from the Center for Whale Research, I put together a little infographic poster about the Southern Resident Killer Whales. If you ever find yourself up around the San Juan Islands, you should try to sneak a peek of them at Lime Kiln Point. It's absolutely incredible to see orcas in the wild.

I really wanted to try my hand at using illustration and a more dynamic bar chart to convey basic demographic information in a way that was easy to understand, and fun to look at. I wound up turning all the assets from this project into a poster.

About me

Hi, I'm Sara!

I like to make things. Pretty things, functional things, things that make people say "wow!" and things that make people want to click to see more. I'm a graphic designer by trade, and I also dabble in illustration and product design. I have a bizarre love of spreadsheets and project management.

I'm an ideas girl; if you have an inkling, I have an interesting and innovative solution to throw at you. I might have five. Only way to find out is to contact me and let me know what you're thinking! I take on commission and contract work whenever there's a project that piques my interest. If you've got something cool, drop me a line and maybe we'll make something beautiful together.

When I'm not sitting behind a computer I'm either out for a hike, watching some whales, or planning my next trip to Ireland. I can sometimes be found tabling at a vendor fair or convention near you. If you want to see my illustration work, you can do that here.
