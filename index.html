Teen Council Rebrand

T-Shirts & Swag.

Teen Council is Planned Parenthood’s award-winning peer education program. Teen Council empowers teens to educate their peers about healthy sexuality and relationship issues. After their logo redesign, they needed new t-shirts and sweatshirts to wear while working as sex educators. The shirts needed to be professional, but also cool enough for high school aged-students to wear and look up to.

The shirts and stickers were intended to promote the new logo, and create buy-in among the teen council members.

Ultimately, we decided to go with a single color t-shirt design, and let the teen council members choose one of the 3 colors in the logo, or charcoal as their shirt color. When they're all together, the colors in the logo are visible and cohesive, but without the price tag of the four color shirts.

About me

I like to make things. Pretty things, functional things, things that make people say "wow!" and things that make people want to click to see more. I'm a graphic designer by trade, but I also dabble in illustration and product design. I have a bizarre love of spreadsheets and project management.

I'm an ideas girl; if you have an inkling, I have an interesting and innovative solution to throw at you. I might have five. Only way to find out is to contact me and let me know what you're thinking! I take on commission and contract work whenever there's a project that piques my interest.

When I'm not sitting behind a computer I'm either out for a hike, watching some whales, or planning my next trip to Ireland. I can sometimes be found tabling at a vendor fair or convention near you. If you want to see my illustration work, you can do that here.
