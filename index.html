Hi, my name is Sara.

I'm a graphic designer & illustrator living in Seattle, Washington.

About Me

I design logos and websites, create brand identities, and am fully fluent in the Adobe Creative Suite and HTML/CSS. I've produced and edited videos and can create motion graphics in After Effects.

I have a passion for visual communication. To me, the most beautiful design is one that is functional. That's why I became a graphic designer--I'm into making art that clearly communicates its intended message. I can make art out of anything from a company newsletter to a press release.

While my illustration skills help in my day-to-day job with wireframing and storyboarding, I'm always happy to put them to work for freelance projects. I've drawn things from e-book covers to posters to children's books. I've also participated in a couple indie game jams with local developers where I learned to create and animate sprites for 2D games. When I'm not working on a project, I'm honing my trivia skills so I can be on Jeopardy.
