Hi, my name is Sara.

About Me

I design. I make graphics for web and print, branding, even a little bit of illustration. I'm fully fluent in the Adobe Creative Suite and I know enough HTML/CSS to make a design into a website. I've produced and edited videos and can create motion graphics in After Effects.

I have a passion for visual communication. To me, the most beautiful design is one that is functional. That's why I became a graphic designer--I'm into making art that clearly communicates its intended message. I can make art out of anything from a company newsletter to a landing page.

While my illustration skills help in my day-to-day job with wireframing and storyboarding, I'm always happy to put them to work for freelance projects. I've drawn e-book covers, posters, and even children's books. I've also participated in a few indie game jams with local developers where I learned to create and animate sprites for 2D games. When I'm not working on a project, I'm honing my trivia skills so I can be on Jeopardy.

"It's Our Right" - Event Branding

For this project, I created branding for a Roe v. Wade anniversary event put on by Planned Parenthood. I created a logo, social media graphics, printed flyers, a program, event signage and posters, and even created buttons that mirrored old school feminist designs.

Planned Parenthood Social Media Graphics

These are various social media graphics created for standalone social media posts during my time at Planned Parenthood.

Title X Infographics

These infographics were created for a social media campaign to educate the public on the benefits of Title X while it was under threat from Congress in the summer of 2015. The first graphic focused on the history of Title X, the second on its relevance to PPGNHI's audience, and the third showed its national significance. This graphic was also used by the national Planned Parenthood social media accounts.

PPGNHI 2015 Holiday Card

I created Planned Parenthood of the Great Northwest and Hawaiian Islands' 2015 holiday card for donors. The trees are the state trees of Alaska, Idaho, Washington, and Hawaii--a way to tie together the four far-flung states. I created the illustrations for each tree and laid out the card and custom envelopes.

Lunette Campaign

I created a landing page and social media ad campaign for a discount code PPGNHI offers for Lunette menstrual cups. I created a series of infographics to higlight the environmental and cost-saving benefits of using a menstrual cup to appeal to our target market. The infographic ads were used on Twitter and Tumblr, while the blue pattern ad was used on Facebook.

#StandwithPP

In Summer 2015, Planned Parenthood was under attack. As part of a nationwide campaign of support, I created brand-compliant materials for Facebook, Twitter, Tumblr, and Instagram for the Planned Parenthood affiliate I worked for. Using pictures from protests and Planned Parenthood branding, I created a social campaign that made it easy for our supporters to show support for us online.

Teen Council Rebrand T-Shirts & Swag

Teen Council is Planned Parenthoodâ€™s award-winning peer education program. Teen Council empowers teens to educate their peers about healthy sexuality and relationship issues. After their logo redesign, they needed new t-shirts and sweatshirts to wear while working as sex educators. The shirts needed to be professional, but also cool enough for high school aged-students to wear and look up to.

The shirts and stickers were intended to promote the new logo, and create buy-in among the teen council members.

Ultimately, we decided to go with a single color t-shirt design, and let the teen council members choose one of the 3 colors in the logo, or charcoal as their shirt color. When they're all together, the colors in the logo are visible and cohesive, but without the price tag of the four color shirts.
